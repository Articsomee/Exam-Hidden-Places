Why Piccolo?

Nestled in the heart of Aarhus Ø, Piccolo brings the charm of an authentic Italian trattoria to Denmark’s vibrant harbour district. With a focus on fresh, high-quality ingredients and traditional recipes, this cozy spot offers everything from hand-tossed pizzas to delicious pastas.
